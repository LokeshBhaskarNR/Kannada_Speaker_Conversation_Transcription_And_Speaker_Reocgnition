ABOUT THE PROJECT

This Speaker Diarization system was developed as part of an internship at Bharat Electronics Limited (BEL) by Lokesh B, SJB Institue Of Technology, under the guidance of Madhukesh U H Sir. The project focuses on identifying and distinguishing different speakers in audio conversations with high accuracy.

Using OpenAI's Whisper model for transcription and SVM classifier for speaker recognition, this system can transcribe audio while tagging different speakers dynamically. It also allows for the addition of new speakers on the go.

The application is built with Flask and supports both pre-recorded audio files and real-time recordings. The processed text includes speaker labels, making it highly useful for meetings, interviews, and forensic applications.
